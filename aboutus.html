Hopeshore Special School located at Kadalundi was founded with the noble objective of empowering the children with special needs who are sidelined from the mainstream of society. The school provides education and therapy to specially-abled children hailing from the lower socio-economic groups. The team at Hopeshore is very supportive and sensitive to the needs of an alarming number of children with special needs who are the victims of negligence. Such children are doubly disadvantaged due to the lack of parental awareness about their needs, social stigma and poor financial access to special education and therapy.

Hopeshore Charitable Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to the rehabilitation of children with special needs. The trust aims at enabling these children to lead a dignified life by catering to their special needs. It has proposed to take up various charitable initiatives to provide services to such children and prevent apathy towards them.

Hopeshore Special School is at the service of children with a wide range of disabilities such as Cerebral Palsy, Autism, mental retardation, learning disability, Attention deficit disorder, Behaviour disorder, speech and language disorders etc. We at Hopeshore offer a complete range of clinical services from pediatric assessment to therapies. Education and therapies like occupational therapy, speech therapy, psychotherapy, physiotherapy and remedial education are provided completely free of cost for underprivileged children.

Psychology

Clinical child psychologists are primarily specialized in understanding the basic psychological needs of children and adolescents and how the family and social contexts influence their cognitive development, socio-emotional adjustment, behavioral adaptation and health status.

Special Education

Special Education focuses on systematically monitored teaching procedures with adapted equipments and materials to help learners with special needs achieve a higher level of personal self-sufficiency and success at school and community

Physio Therapy

Pediatric physiotherapy deals with the treatment of children who need range of motion, strength, coordination, postural control, quality of movement and gross motor development. Paediatric physiotherapy plays an integral role in comprehensive rehabilitation of children with Cerebral palsy, Delayed milestones, Congenital syndromes,Traumatic brain injury, Premature birth or Any post surgical orthopedic conditions Early diagnosis and treatment help children to improve their functional mobility & strength making them more independent.

Speech Therapy

Speech-language therapy helps to coordinate the mechanics of speech with the social use of language. Speech therapy focuses on:

Ability to understand language

Ability to use ideas to express

When a speech or language problem is identified and treated early in a child, it is unlikely that the problem will persist or get worse
